7th convocation of the eastern africa statistical training center of the year 2024 continues the traditition with in the the academic institutions of gathering together academicians,students,alumn,benefactor and industries proffessional as the stakeholders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on topics that touch teaching,research,and proffession.as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year,18 november African statistics day.
African statistics day is celebrated each year on 18th of november. the commemoration aims among others. to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic develomet.
In this year year convocation aims to understand the African continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and the potental role statistics and big data in discovering markets and serving customers accross Africa

AWARDING OF VARIOUS PRIZE AND GIFT

There will be a ceremony for awarding various prizes and gifts presented by the guest of honor on the event day to graduates who have excelled in different areas of their studies.

Additionally, these graduates who have performed well will have the opportunity to walk in front of the guest of honor and take a photo together with the guest of honor.
